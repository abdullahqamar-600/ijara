## Business model — synthesized

**What ijaraCDC does:**
ijara Community Development Corp. is a 20-year-old (founded 2005) Sharia-compliant home and business financing facilitator for Muslims in the **USA and Canada**. They offer an **interest-free (riba-free)** alternative to conventional mortgages, structured around the Islamic **ijara** (lease-to-own) contract.

**How "ijara" works (in plain English):**
- A funding entity (investor / co-op) purchases the property the client wants.
- The client signs a lease agreement and makes monthly payments.
- A portion of each payment buys equity in the property; the remainder is rent.
- At end of term, full ownership transfers to the client.
- No interest accrues at any point — making it halal under Islamic finance principles.

**Product lines:**
- Residential home financing
- Commercial property financing
- Non-profit / mosque / Islamic-center financing
- **Conversion** — switching an existing conventional mortgage to a halal structure
- **Down Payment Assistance** — up to 2.5% grant
- **ijara 360 App** — customer-facing tool to manage their financing lifestyle

**Audience:**
- Practicing Muslims in the US & Canada who want to own a home or business but cannot use interest-based products.
- Both English- and French-speaking (bilingual market — meaningful for Canada).

**Trust signals they lean on:**
- 20 years in business
- Thousands of families served
- Sharia compliance / fatwa documentation
- Personal relationships ("you can talk to people who understand the business")
- Investor-led structure
