Questions, answered.

The three most common questions, or one call to a halal-finance advisor.

Frequently asked

Is ijara financing really interest-free? +
Yes. The ijara structure is a lease-to-own arrangement. A co-op of investors purchases your property outright; you then make monthly payments that are a combination of fair rent and equity buy-in. At no point does interest accrue.
How is ijaraCDC different from a conventional mortgage? +
A conventional mortgage charges interest (riba) on borrowed money. With ijara, the investor co-op owns the property and you lease it while progressively buying equity. There is no loan and no interest. Your monthly amount is also predictable.
What's the minimum down payment? +
Most residential programs start at 10–20%. Our USDA program offers 100% financing in eligible areas, and our Down Payment Assistance can grant up to 2.5% toward your share.
