How it works

The Process is Very Simple

Step 01 of 05

We create a Trust

A dedicated Trust is established for your transaction, giving you a clear, transparent legal structure built around your faith from day one.

Scholar reviewed Transparent
Step 02 of 05

The Trust Owns the Property

Our co op of investors funds the Trust, which purchases the home or property outright on your behalf. You never take on debt or interest.

Residential Commercial
Step 03 of 05

You have a Lease to Own Agreement

You sign a clear lease to own agreement with the Trust. The terms are predictable, fully halal, and reviewed by scholars from start to finish.

Clear terms Zero riba
Step 04 of 05

Each Month you make a Rent Payment

You make one predictable monthly payment to the Trust. It is fair rent for the home you live in or the property you use, with no interest involved.

One monthly payment Fair rent
Step 05 of 05

Part of your Payment goes to ownership until you Own 100%

A portion of every payment buys you more equity in the property. Month by month your ownership grows until full title is yours — completely halal, completely yours.

Equity grows monthly Full ownership
